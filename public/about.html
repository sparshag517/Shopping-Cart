ABOUT

We are a music enthusiast group from the city of Bareilly in Uttar Pradesh (UP 25) and are on the mission to spread peace through music. Every band member is immensely talented in their field of instrument.

We have Yashvi Gaglani on the keyboard, Sparsh Agarwal handling the vocals and the guitar, while Bhavya Chahal on the drums.
